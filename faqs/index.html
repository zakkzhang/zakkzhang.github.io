Frequently asked questions

General

General

Who is Hello?

Hello is your personal travel assistant to book hotels and flights, and to arrange any other travel needs you may have. Simply send us a message like “SFO to Boston next Friday for three nights” via Facebook Messenger and we will get it arranged for you.

What can I do with Hello?

Chat with your travel buddy one-on-one over Facebook Messenger and message us any of your travel needs, from flight and hotel bookings, train and restaurant reservations to recommendations on things to do and itinerary planning.

When and where is Hello operating?

Hello is available anytime, anywhere 24/7 around the globe. Add us on Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram to get updates!

How much does it cost?

We have two current plans, Hello Plus and Hello. Hello Plus costs $9.99 / month or $100.00 / year, while Hello is a free service. Look here for more details.

Service

Service

How does the service work?

Think about us as your travel buddy, who you can chat with on Facebook Messager. Tell us your travel needs and we’ll take care of it!

Are you a bot?

Hello is currently powered by human travel buddies and artificial intelligence software which help to make your travel seamless.

Does Hello offers the best prices?

We do our best to find you the most competitive prices for all your bookings! However, if you find a cheaper price, let us know and we’ll book it on that price for you.

Do you charge for each booking?

We do not charge by per booking, but offer a subscription plan for our Hello Plus customers.

How do you charge me?

I’ll collect your payment information to make purchases on your behalf. This payment information is treated with high-security - we use the same 256-bit encryption and security standards as large financial institutions.

Company

Tell me more about the team?

Hello is founded by a team from Google, Apple, Stanford, Google and MIT, and comprise of diverse individuals of engineers, designers, and hustlers.

Where are you located?

We are located right next to Stanford University, in Palo Alto at the heart of Silicon Valley.

Why are you working on this?

We are a close-knit tribe with a shared passion for technology, travel, and AI. Collectively, we have traveled to more than 150 cities and 80 countries, and speak over 10 languages. We want to transform travel to make it as simple as messaging a friend!
